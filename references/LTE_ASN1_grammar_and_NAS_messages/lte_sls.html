LCS Application Protocol

29.171 v11.3.0 (2013-06)

Location services in E-UTRAN applicable to the SLs interface between the E-SMLC and the MME

  • Convey LCS-AP messages and parameters between the MME to the E-SMLC
  • Tunnel LTE Positioning Protocols :
    • LPP between the E-SMLC and the target UE
    • LPPa between the E-SMLC and the eNB
